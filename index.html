About Us

Established in 2024, our restaurant is a celebration of the rich culture and flavors of Brazil

We believe that a meal is more than just food; it is an experience. That is why we are dedicated to offering a welcoming environment for all ages, always with the best dishes that represent the diversity of Brazil combined with the Canadian hospitality.
